About Me

Hi, I'm Grace Munyiri, a Data Science and AI professional with expertise in machine learning, data analytics, and risk modeling. I am passionate about leveraging data-driven insights to solve complex business challenges and drive innovation. As the founder of Cacumator Mentorship, I empower young African girls by providing access to education, career guidance, and mentorship opportunities.

I'm an accomplished Data Science and AI professional with a background in advanced analytics, actuarial science, financial services, and compliance. My expertise spans machine learning, risk modeling, AI-driven decision-making, and data visualization, making me a sought-after professional in insurance, fintech, and risk analysis. I have held international roles as a Data Scientist, Actuarial Data Analyst, and Pension Data Analyst, where I have contributed to innovative solutions in predictive analytics, financial forecasting, and operational research.

Beyond my technical expertise, I am a multi-award-winning mentor, keynote speaker, and advocate for women in technology and finance. I am the founder of Cacumator Mentorship, an initiative dedicated to equipping young African girls with education, career mentorship, and leadership skills. Through this platform, I have facilitated mentorship programs, built strategic partnerships with schools, and provided young girls with opportunities to access STEM careers.

My passion lies in harnessing AI and machine learning to drive meaningful change while mentoring the next generation of women in tech and finance.
